How to use this site

Research. Educate yourself. Keep living.

Research.

Learn what ingredients actually are before judging them by name. Many scientific sounding compounds occur naturally in fruits and foods.

Educate yourself.

Everything is made of chemicals, including water, apples, plants, and the human body. Context matters.

Keep living.

Learning about ingredients should make life clearer, not more stressful. The goal is understanding, not anxiety.
