How to use this site

Research. Educate yourself. Keep living.

Research.

Look at what ingredients actually are before judging them by name. Many scientific sounding compounds occur naturally in everyday foods.

Educate yourself.

Everything is made of chemicals, including water, fruit, and your body. The important question is context, amount, and purpose.

Keep living.

Learning about ingredients should make life clearer, not more stressful. The goal is understanding, not anxiety.
